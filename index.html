About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in Computational Psychiatry at the University of Alberta under the supervision of Dr. Bo Cao. During my PhD training at the University of Manitoba, I was fortunate to be advised by Dr. Zahra Moussavi. My multidisciplinary research focuses on mental health and neurodegenerative disease, image and signal analysis, statistical analysis, and machine learning.
