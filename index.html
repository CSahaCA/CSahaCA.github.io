About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in Computational Psychiatry at the University of Alberta. My research focuses on mental health and neurodegenerative disease, image and signal analysis, statistical analysis, and machine learning, supported by multidisciplinary collaboration with healthcare professionals, engineers, and computer scientists.
